About SceneCraft AI

SceneCraft AI is an advanced cinematic analysis tool built to help writers and filmmakers elevate their storytelling. It offers:

  • Pacing Analysis: Understand and optimize the flow of your scene.
  • Character Stakes: Deep dive into motivations and emotional beats.
  • Dialogue Subtext: Uncover what's left unsaid.
  • Visual Suggestions: Cinematography ideas to amplify themes.
  • Creative Prompts: Spark new angles with "what if" scenarios.
